Privacy Policy for Zakkir

Last updated: June 2026

Zakkir is an All-in-one islamic app for daily worship. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Access

Location Information

The app requests approximate location (coarse location) permission while in use for:

  • Calculating prayer times
  • Determining Qiblah direction

Your location is processed locally on your device and may be stored on your device for convenience. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data to our servers.

Audio Files

The app may download Quran audio files (recitations) to your device so they can be played offline. These files are stored locally on your device.

Saved Images

The app may allow saving images to your device gallery using Android system features.

2. Advertising (AdMob & Mediation Partners)

The app displays advertisements including banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads using Unity Ads.

Which may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)
  • IP address (used for general location estimation)
  • App interaction and ad performance data

This data is used for:

  • Serving relevant ads
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Preventing fraud

These services operate under their own privacy policies:

  • Unity Ads:
    https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

The developer does not have direct access to data collected by these advertising providers.

Consent: In regions where required (such as the European Economic Area), users may be asked to provide consent for personalized or non-personalized ads.

3. Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

The app uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google, to collect anonymous usage data automatically. This includes:

  • App open and session data (first open, session start, session duration)
  • App version and update events
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, country)
  • User engagement metrics (time spent in app)
  • Ad interaction events (impressions, clicks, rewards) linked to AdMob

This data is collected anonymously and is used solely to understand how the app is used and to improve the user experience. No personally identifiable information is collected through this service.

Firebase Analytics operates under Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We do not collect, sell, or share personal data ourselves.

However, third-party advertising and analytics partners (such as Unity Ads, and Firebase Analytics) may process data independently as described above.

5. Data Storage

App settings, downloaded audio, and saved preferences remain stored locally on your device. Analytics and advertising data collected by third-party services are stored on their own servers in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. However, third-party ad and analytics services may collect data as described in this policy.

7. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services including:

  • Unity Ads (advertising via mediation)
  • Google Firebase Analytics (usage analytics)

Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
